Cash-Money

19 Feb, 2015 · Read in about 1 min · (157 Words)

Cash-Money was a game I developed for the mobile development club. It was an idea I had while messing around with drag-and-drop functionality in Android. Basically, the user starts a game, then gold and silver coins start falling from the top of the screen. The goal is to drag as many of them as possible into the corresponding piggy banks at the bottom of the screen before they fall out of the screen’s window.

The player gets three lives, and every time a coin falls out, they lose one. As time progresses, the coins start falling faster and faster. At the end of the game, the user’s high score is tracked.

I developed this game before I learned about OpenGL, and so it’s a little hacky, but it works. Now that I understand OpenGL ES, I’d like to someday redo this app properly, giving it some more functionality and some images & icons that don’t look atrocious.
