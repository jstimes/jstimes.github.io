Sr. Design Project Details

15 Sep, 2016 · Read in about 2 min · (231 Words)

Senior Design is a year long course that all software, computer, and electrical engineers must take. My team consists of 6 software engineers. We have a project advisor who is a professor at Iowa State studying the therapeutic benefits that gardening can have on female inmates across Iowa. Her and her team have developed training materials and guides for the inmates so they can learn about all aspects of the garden lifecycle (planting, nurturing, harvest, post-harvest, etc.).

Our team is tasked with developing an Android application so inmates can take the learning materials into the garden with them, and have instant access to the information they need. Additionally, it will provide them with ways to analyze their progress. Ideally they could take pictures of plants that aren’t looking healthy, and they will be sent to our advisor so she can help identify problems.

Another big aspect of this project is the security. Iowa has some progressive prison systems that are willing to allow inmates access to tablets, but their usage must be very restrictive. For this reason, we will most likely modify the Android operating system to restrict a lot of functionality. This presents our team with an interesting challenge, as we must restrict Wifi access, but still allow data to be sent back to our advisor when the tablets are docked.

This page will be updated as the project progresses.
